# Rogue Miners

> **A strategic worker-placement board game of mining, guilds, theft, and reputation.**

## Overview

**Rogue Miners** is a competitive board game for **2–4 players** where rival mining guilds fight for wealth and prestige in a dangerous underground world. During the day, players manage their workforce, mine valuable gems, negotiate with powerful guilds, and expand their economic engine. When night falls, thieves emerge from the shadows to steal resources, sabotage opponents, and manipulate the balance of power.

The game combines **worker placement**, **resource management**, **engine building**, **deck building**, and **high player interaction** into a tactical experience where every decision has consequences.

## Gameplay

Each round is divided into four phases:

### Planning

* Draw action cards
* Roll and assign miners
* Prepare day and night actions
* Perform free actions

### Day Shift

Players resolve actions across five daily transitions:

* Sunrise
* Morning
* Noon
* Afternoon
* Sunset

During these shifts they can:

* Mine gems
* Complete Bank Contracts
* Recruit Bandits
* Visit the Market
* Recruit Thieves
* Complete Tavern activities

### Night Shift

The underground becomes far more dangerous.

Players activate thief cards to:

* Steal gems
* Sabotage opponents
* Guard their own operations
* Increase or reduce their reputation

### Clearing

The board is refreshed, markets restocked, and the next round begins.

## Project Goals

The goal of Rogue Miners is to create a highly interactive strategy game where players constantly balance:

* Economy vs Risk
* Growth vs Reputation
* Cooperation vs Sabotage

Every decision should feel meaningful, while every player remains engaged throughout the game.
