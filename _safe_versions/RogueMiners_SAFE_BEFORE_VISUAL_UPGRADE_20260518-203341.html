A moonlit mine board game
ROGUE MINERS
Beneath the five shifting suns, miners gamble their honor for gems, gold, and a rumor of forgotten crowns. By day, crews race through the tunnels, market stalls, taverns, and thieves' dens. By night, guards are posted, traps are set, and quiet hands reach for treasure that was never theirs. Choose your crew, watch your reputation, and leave the mines with more prestige than suspicion.
Setup
For 3 players, the last action spot in every board row is crossed out. For 2 players, the last two spots are crossed out and unavailable.
